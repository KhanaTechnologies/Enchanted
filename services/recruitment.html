Recuitment
Services

The Enchanting Corporation takes pleasure in the complete business solutions we provide depending on the unique business labor requirements of our clients. We meet the needs of our clients by assigning suitable, competent contract and temporary workers to a location for the duration of a contract. In various industries, we provide contract workers on short and long terms.

Additionally, we collaborate with clients to identify peak staffing patterns and forecast the rise in staffing requirements in order to efficiently match personnel with workload. We do this to make sure that employers won't be short on staff or concerned about hiring more people for ambiguous lengths of time or quick projects.

Additionally, as Enchanted as we are, we go above and beyond the call of duty by assembling teams of specialists to handle tasks like staff transportation to various sites, employee training, PPE (Personal Protective Equipment), medical examinations, inductions, lodging arrangements, and many other services clients may need..
